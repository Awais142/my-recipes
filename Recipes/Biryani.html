BIRYANI

Description

This is a detailed recipe of authentic Pakistani Chicken Biryani that is very delicious and easy to make. No, you don't need tons of difficult-to-find spices or ready-made masala. This is chicken biryani that fills your house with a tempting biryani aroma.

ehold! An authentic Chicken Biryani recipe with simple, easy-to-follow instructions (no curveballs!) and mouthwatering, traditional Pakistani and Indian flavor.

This recipe includes tips on how to get fluffy rice, tender chicken, and the distinct biryani taste. Tested to perfection!

Ingredients

  • (750 gram) chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoon green chilli paste
  • 3 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder or 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • ¾ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 inch cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1½ teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 black cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon salt , or to taste
